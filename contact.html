Community Guidelines

Our community thrives because of the passion and respect our members show for each other and for the How to Train Your Dragon franchise. We welcome fans of all ages, backgrounds, and levels of familiarity with the series. Whether you prefer the animated films or the live-action adaptation (or love them both!), there's room for everyone here.

We ask that all community members engage respectfully, avoid spoilers without proper warnings, and help create an environment where everyone feels welcome to share their love of dragons. Together, we can build a community that reflects the best values of the franchise: friendship, understanding, and the courage to be yourself.
