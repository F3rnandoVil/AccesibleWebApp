Welcome to the World of Dragons

For generations, the people of Berk lived in fear of dragons. These magnificent creatures were seen as dangerous enemies, raiding our villages and stealing our livestock. But everything changed when a young Viking named Hiccup discovered the truth: dragons aren't our enemies—they're our friends, our companions, and our partners in adventure.

The How to Train Your Dragon franchise has captured hearts worldwide through its stunning animated trilogy and the recent live-action adaptation. Each telling of this beloved story brings something unique while maintaining the core message: understanding and friendship can bridge even the widest gaps. From the original 2010 animated masterpiece to the 2025 live-action reimagining, the bond between Hiccup and Toothless continues to inspire audiences of all ages.

The Journey Begins

The story of How to Train Your Dragon follows Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, the unlikely hero of Berk. As the son of the village chief, Stoick the Vast, Hiccup was expected to be a mighty dragon slayer. Instead, he became the first Viking to befriend a dragon—a Night Fury he named Toothless. This friendship changed everything, not just for Hiccup, but for the entire Viking world.

Through three animated films released in 2010, 2014, and 2019, we watched Hiccup grow from an awkward teenager into a confident leader. The trilogy explored themes of identity, friendship, responsibility, and letting go. In 2025, a live-action adaptation brought this world to life in a new way, introducing these timeless characters to a new generation while honoring the original story that fans have cherished for years.

Exploring the Dragon Universe

The world of How to Train Your Dragon extends far beyond the films. The franchise includes multiple television series, short films, games, and books that expand the mythology of dragons and Vikings. Whether you're discovering this world for the first time through the live-action film or you've been a fan since the original animated movie, there's always something new to explore in this rich, vibrant universe.

On this site, you'll find comprehensive information about both the animated trilogy and the live-action adaptation. Learn about the different films and their unique approaches to telling this beloved story, discover the various dragon species that populate this world, and connect with fellow fans who share your passion for this extraordinary franchise. Whether you're Team Animated or excited about the live-action version (or both!), you'll find a home here in our community.
